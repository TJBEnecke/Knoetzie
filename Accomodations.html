Our Accomodations

Die Melkstal Mensions

Each room can accommodate 2 adults and 1 child up to the age of 12. The rooms are furnished with a double bed and a single bed, the bathroom for each room is fitted with a shower, toilet and a basin.


A basic kitchenette is equipped with heat and eat facilities, including a bar fridge and microwave. All the rooms have access to a communal kitchen for preparing of food as well as outdoor braai facilities. There is a TV with selected satellite channels and Wi-Fi.


The rooms open up to a private verandah with a table and chairs.

Die Washuis

Die Washuis, the smaller cottage, can accommodate 3 guests, 2x adults and 1 child. There is a bedroom/living area with a king size bed and also a day bed in the bedroom area.


The cottage has a fully equipped kitchen (gas plates) and a separate bathroom fitted with a shower, basin and toilet. An inverter keeps the lights on during loadshedding.
