About Us

Knoetzie Kamma offers a peaceful getaway.

Accommodation is offered in 3 cottages and 5 rooms. There are 2 cottages, each with 2 bedrooms, a lounge area, a bathroom with a shower, and a kitchen with a fridge, microwave, stove and an oven. The third cottage has a bedroom, a bathroom with a shower and a kitchen equipped with a fridge, microwave oven and stove.


Knoetzie Kamma rooms each have a double bed and a single bed and offer sleeping space for 2 adults and 1 child up to 12 years old. The en suite bathroom has a shower.


Other facilities include a TV with DStv, WiFi, a kitchenette with basic heating and dining facilities, such as a small fridge and a microwave. All guests have access to a communal kitchen and outdoor braai facilities. Each unit has a private veranda or balcony with a table and chairs.


Small, trained pets with their own bedding will be allowed if arranged in advance. There are braai facilities on site and pets are allowed on request. Guests can explore the town and all it has to offer, or drive along the Garden Route and visit one of the many beaches in the area.
